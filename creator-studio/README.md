# Creator Studio

Operating system for a solo creator running a personal brand + content engine + digital product ladder.

Cascade = writing partner, launch strategist, audience-growth co-pilot.

---

## First Steps

1. **Fill `CONTEXT/project-brief.md`** — niche, past-self avatar, offer ladder, North Star metric
2. **Fill `CONTEXT/voice-guide.md`** — your voice rules (use jackbutcher.md in KNOWLEDGE/ as model)
3. **Fill `CONTEXT/positioning.md`** — unique message + who you repel/attract
4. **Seed `BRAND/pillars.md`** — 3–5 topic pillars (no more)
5. **Skim `BRAND/banned-words.md`** — add words that are dead to your voice
6. **Drop first 10 ideas into `CONTENT/idea-bank.md`**

Then start drafting.

---

## What Cascade Does Here

- Drafts tweets / threads / newsletters / emails / sales pages to your voice
- Generates 5-hook options across 5 frames on demand
- Reverse-engineers swipe-file posts into frames and rewrites
- Runs voice/curiosity/contrast/closing gates on every draft
- Manages the idea → draft → scheduled → published pipeline
- Writes product briefs, outlines, sales pages, launch emails
- Extracts lessons from `CONTENT/published/` into `post-mortems.md`

## What Cascade Does NOT Do Here

- Publish anything externally (your job)
- Change your voice, avatar, or positioning without asking
- Price products unilaterally
- Invent craft rules (references KNOWLEDGE/ only)
