# Email-Based Course (EBC)

> Build a structured learning experience delivered entirely through email — an evergreen product you build once and improve over time.

## What This Template Is For

An **Email-Based Course** is a course delivered through automated email sequences. Unlike a newsletter (which requires ongoing creation), an EBC is a product — finite, evergreen, and built to run without you after launch.

**Choose your model before building:**

| Model | Price | Goal | Landing Page |
|-------|-------|------|-------------|
| **Demand Gen** | Free or $5–$20 | Drive to a higher-ticket offer | Light — low commitment needed |
| **Standalone Product** | $5–$230 | Direct course revenue | Strong — needs testimonials + outcome promise |

## The 80/20 of Building an EBC

**80% of the outcome comes from:**
- Getting the outline right (most thinking required)
- Writing compelling body copy (most effort required)

**20% of the outcome comes from:**
- Email automations, templates, and design (mostly administrative)

**Build order:** Outline → Content → Automations → Design

---

## Quick-Start

### Step 1: Define the goal
What should the reader be able to DO after finishing this course?

### Step 2: Choose a depth approach
- **Horizontal** — key lessons across multiple areas (good for overview + multiple upsell targets)
- **Vertical** — deep on one area, tease the others (good for one flagship service)
- **101** — most basic things before going deeper (good when you have products above it)

### Step 3: Outline using the 7 questions
1. What am I talking about?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What do they need to know first?
4. What are the primary pillars?
5. What are the subtopics of those pillars?
6. In each subtopic: considerations, challenges, best practices?
7. How do they put the knowledge into action?

### Step 4: Map your email types
See `DESIGN/email-archetypes.md` — the 7 email types and when to use each.

### Step 5: Write using the 8-step body copy structure
See `DESIGN/body-copy-structure.md` — the framework for every deep dive email.

### Step 6: Build automations (last)
- Platform: Mailerlite or ConvertKit
- 2 templates: plain text (welcome only) + designed (all others)
- Fast Forward links for self-pacing
- Weekend skip automation

## Course Length Rules

| Emails | Structure Needed |
|--------|-----------------|
| < 15 emails | Welcome + Deep Dives + Action Item + CTA |
| ≥ 15 emails | Add Chapter Overviews, Quizzes, and Feedback emails between chapters |

**Subject line format:**
- Multi-chapter: `[#.#] Subject Line`
- Single-chapter: `[Lesson 1] Subject Line`

## Monetization Paths

| Path | Description |
|------|------------|
| **Lead gen for service** | Free EBC → demonstrates expertise → sells your service |
| **Lead gen for premium course** | Low-cost EBC → proves your methodology → sells $997+ program |
| **Standalone product** | $5–$230 direct purchase |
| **Affiliate inside** | Free EBC with affiliate links for recommended tools |
| **White-label** | License the content to other publishers |

---

## Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|----------|
| Open rate | > 40% (email courses outperform newsletters) |
| Completion rate | > 60% (tracks how many finish all emails) |
| Fast forward usage | Indicates highly engaged readers |
| Upsell conversion | 3–8% of completers (varies by offer) |
| Review/feedback response | > 20% on ask emails |
