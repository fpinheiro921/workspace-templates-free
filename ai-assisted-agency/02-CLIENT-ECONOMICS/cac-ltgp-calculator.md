# CAC / LTGP Calculator

## The Business Equation

All revenue in any business comes down to two numbers:

```
CAC  = Cost to Acquire a Customer (time + money)
LTGP = Lifetime Gross Profit per customer
ROI  = LTGP ÷ CAC
```

**Note on time:** Time is a form of money. If you spend 4 hours getting a client, and your effective hourly rate is $250, your CAC is $1,000 — even if you spent $0 on ads.

---

## Step 1: Calculate Your LTGP

```
Monthly Revenue per Client:    $________
Gross Margin (fulfillment %):  ________% 
Monthly Gross Profit:          $________ (Revenue × Margin)
Average Retention (months):    ________
LTGP:                          $________ (Monthly GP × Retention)
```

**Example:**
```
Monthly Revenue:    $2,000
Gross Margin:       80%
Monthly GP:         $1,600
Avg Retention:      5 months
LTGP:               $8,000
```

---

## Step 2: Calculate Your CAC

```
Hours spent on outreach + sales per client acquired: ________ hrs
Your effective hourly value (replace yourself cost): $________/hr
Time-based CAC:                                      $________

Ad spend per client acquired (if running ads):       $________

Total CAC:                                           $________
```

**Example:**
```
Hours to close 1 client: 4 hrs
Effective hourly rate:    $250/hr
Time CAC:                 $1,000
Ad spend:                 $0
Total CAC:                $1,000
```

---

## Step 3: Calculate Your ROI

```
ROI = LTGP ÷ CAC = ________ × return
```

**Example:** $8,000 ÷ $1,000 = **8x ROI**

Rule of thumb: Anything above 3x is a healthy business. Below 1x and you're losing money.

---

## Step 4: Model Your LTV Stack

Adding upsells and downsells dramatically changes LTGP without requiring new clients.

### Base Case
```
LTGP (no upsells/downsells):   $8,000
```

### Add Upsell
```
Upsell price/month:             $2,000
% of clients who take it:       40%
Upsell retention (months):      5
Additional LTGP from upsell:    $2,000 × 80% margin × 5 months × 40% uptake
                              = $3,200 added to average LTGP
New average LTGP:               $11,200  (+40%)
```

### Add Downsell
```
Downsell price/month:           $1,000
% of churning clients who take: 40%
Downsell retention (months):    5
Additional LTGP from downsell:  $1,000 × 80% × 5 × 40% uptake
                              = $1,600 added to average LTGP
New average LTGP:               $12,800  (+60% vs base)
```

**Key insight:** You went from $8,000 to $12,800 LTGP (60% increase) without signing a single new client. This means you can outspend competitors on acquisition, take home more profit, and work less.

---

## Step 5: Payback Period

How many months until you recover your CAC?

```
Payback Period = CAC ÷ Monthly Gross Profit
```

**Example:** $1,000 CAC ÷ $1,600/month GP = **0.625 months** (under 1 month)

At scale (with paid ads), payback period often goes negative in month 1. That's normal — it gets paid back over the retention period. The math still works if LTGP >> CAC.

## What These Numbers Tell You

**If ROI < 3x:** Your offer is priced wrong, your retention is too low, or your CAC is too high. Fix before scaling.

**If Payback Period > 3 months:** Cash flow will be tight when scaling. Consider shortening commitment or taking upfront payments.

**If Gross Margin < 60%:** You're spending too much on fulfillment. AI automation should push this to 70–85%.
