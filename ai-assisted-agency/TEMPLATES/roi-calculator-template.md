## Section 2: LTGP (Lifetime Gross Profit per Client)

```
Monthly revenue per client:            $________
× Gross margin %:                      × ________%
= Monthly gross profit per client:     $________

× Average retention (months):          × ________
= LTGP (Lifetime Gross Profit):        $________
```

**Example:**
```
$2,000/month × 80% margin × 5 months = $8,000 LTGP
```

## Section 4: ROI Calculation

```
Your monthly fee:                      $________
× Commitment period (months):          × ________
= Total investment:                    $________

Total new gross profit generated:      $________
÷ Total investment:                    ÷ $________
= ROI multiple:                        ________x
```

**Benchmark:**
- Below 3x: Fix the offer or reduce the price
- 3–5x: Solid. Present with confidence.
- 5–10x: Strong. Use this as your lead number.
- 10x+: Make sure the math is right. If it is, say it plainly.

## Section 5: Payback Period

```
Your monthly fee:                      $________
÷ Monthly gross profit delivered:      ÷ $________
= Months to break even:                ________ months
```

Under 1 month = very strong close. Over 3 months = address the cash flow objection proactively.

## Talk Track (use during the call)

```
"Let me show you how we think about the return on this.

You said your average client is worth about $[contract value] 
and they typically stay [retention] months.

At [margin]% gross margin, that's $[LTGP] in lifetime value per client.

If we get you [N] new clients over [timeframe], 
that's $[N × LTGP] in new gross profit.

Our fee over that same period is $[price × months].

That's a [ROI]x return.

Does that math make sense for [company]?"
```
