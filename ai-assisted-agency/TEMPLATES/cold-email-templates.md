# Cold Email Templates

Copy-paste starting points. Replace all [brackets] before sending.
Every template uses spintax `{option1|option2|option3}` for subject lines.

---

## Template 1: Lead Gen Agency (B2B SaaS)

**ICP:** CEO/Head of Sales at B2B SaaS, 10–100 employees, actively hiring

**Email 1:**
```
Subject: {quick idea for [company]|[first name] — pipeline thought|leads for [company]}

[Personalized first line from LinkedIn activity]

We build AI-driven lead gen systems for B2B SaaS companies 
that generate 20+ qualified demo calls/month.

Done it for [N] companies similar to [company] in the last 6 months.

Worth a 15-min call to see if the math works for you?

[Your name]
```

**Email 2 (Day 3):**
```
Subject: {what [similar company] did|following up|quick case study}

Following up on my last note.

[Client name], a [X]-person SaaS company, went from 
[old number] to [new number] qualified calls/month in [timeframe].

Given you're [signal from research — hiring AEs / launching new product], 
thought the timing might be right.

Open to a quick chat this week?

[Your name]
```

**Email 3 (Day 5):**
```
Subject: {closing the loop|last one|no hard feelings}

Last one — promise.

If consistent pipeline isn't a priority right now, totally understood.

If it ever becomes one, feel free to reach out.

[Your name]
```

## Template 2: Content Agency (LinkedIn + Newsletter)

**Email 1:**

We help [niche] founders publish a consistent week of content 
— LinkedIn posts, email newsletter, and video scripts — 
all written in their voice, without them writing a word.

Takes about 2 minutes of their time per week (a voice note).

Curious if that's something worth a quick conversation about?

[Your name]

**Email 2 (Day 3):**
```
Subject: {how [similar person] grew to [result]|content system|follow up}

Sent you a note a few days ago.

[Name], a [role] in [industry], went from posting once a month 
to 5x/week — his LinkedIn went from [X] to [Y] followers 
and he closed [N] clients from inbound in [timeframe].

All without writing anything himself.

Worth 15 minutes to see if it applies to your situation?

[Your name]
```

**Email 3 (Day 5):**
```
Subject: {last note|closing the loop|[first name]}

Last follow-up.

If LinkedIn and email growth aren't a priority right now, 
no worries at all.

If they ever are — you know where to find me.

[Your name]
```

## Personalized First Line Bank

Use when Apify doesn't find recent posts. Pull from LinkedIn bio, job history, or company news.

```
"Saw [company] just [recent news — raised / launched / hired] — congrats on [specific thing]."

"Noticed you've been building out the [department] team at [company] — sounds like [inference]."

"Your [specific post topic] last [timeframe] was spot on — we've seen the same pattern."

"[Company]'s [product/service] caught my attention — [specific observation]."

"Congrats on the [funding round / launch / award] — [specific detail]."
```

## Subject Line Swipe File

**Direct / Value:**
- "20 calls/month for [company]"
- "[first name] — pipeline idea"
- "leads for [company name]"

**Curiosity:**
- "quick question"
- "had a thought about [company]"
- "noticed something about [company]"

**Social proof:**
- "how [similar company] hit [result]"
- "[N] clients in [timeframe]"
- "what [name] did differently"

**Personalized:**
- "[first name] — [reference to their content]"
- "re: [company]'s [specific initiative]"
- "congrats on [recent news]"

**Follow-up:**
- "following up"
- "closing the loop"
- "last one"
- "no hard feelings"
