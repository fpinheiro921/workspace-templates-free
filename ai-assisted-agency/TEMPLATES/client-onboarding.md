# Client Onboarding Template

The first 7 days set the tone for the entire engagement. Move fast, communicate clearly, deliver something tangible in week 1.

---

## Day 0: Contract Signed

Send within 2 hours of closing:

```
Subject: Welcome to [Agency Name] — next steps

[First name],

Excited to get started. Here's what happens next:

1. You'll receive an onboarding questionnaire — takes ~15 minutes
2. We'll book a kickoff call for [date/time range]
3. Week 1 deliverable: [specific thing — e.g., first prospect list of 50 ICPs]

Questionnaire link: [link]
Kickoff calendar link: [link]

Any questions before then, just reply here.

[Your name]
```

---

## Onboarding Questionnaire

Send via Typeform, Notion form, or Google Form.

**Section 1: Business Context**
```
- What is the primary outcome you're hiring us for?
- What does a "successful client" look like for your business?
- What's your average contract value? Average retention?
- Who is your ideal client? (industry, size, title, geography)
- Who is NOT a good fit? (clients you don't want)
```

**Section 2: Current Acquisition**
```
- How are you currently getting clients?
- What's worked? What hasn't?
- Have you done cold email or LinkedIn outreach before? Results?
- Do you have case studies or proof points we can use?
```

**Section 3: Messaging**
```
- What is your core offer in one sentence?
- What pain does it solve?
- What result does a client get in the first 30/60/90 days?
- Who are your top 3 competitors?
- Why do clients choose you over them?
```

**Section 4: Logistics**
```
- Who is our primary point of contact?
- Preferred communication method (email / Slack / WhatsApp)?
- When can we expect replies during the week?
- Are there any blackout dates or major business events this quarter?
```

## Kickoff Call Agenda (45 min)

```
[0–5 min]   Introductions + agenda overview
[5–15 min]  Review questionnaire answers — clarify anything unclear
[15–25 min] Define success metrics for Month 1
[25–35 min] Walk through delivery process step by step
[35–40 min] Set communication cadence (weekly update format/time)
[40–45 min] Q&A + confirm next milestone
```

**What to confirm on the call:**
- [ ] ICP is locked (we won't start prospecting until this is agreed)
- [ ] Offer language is agreed (the one-liner we'll use in emails)
- [ ] Proof points are collected (case studies, testimonials, results)
- [ ] Access granted (Google Sheets shared, email domain confirmed)
- [ ] Week 1 deliverable agreed in writing

---

## Week 1 Checklist (Agency Side)

```
- [ ] Prospect list built (50–100 contacts minimum)
- [ ] Email infrastructure set up (domain, SPF/DKIM/DMARC)
- [ ] Warm-up started on sending inbox
- [ ] 3 email copy variants written and shared for client review
- [ ] Google Sheet created and shared with client
- [ ] First status update sent (see template below)
```

## Month 1 Review Template

At the end of Month 1, run a brief review call and send a summary.

**Agenda:**
```
[0–10 min]  Review Month 1 numbers vs agreed targets
[10–20 min] What's working, what needs adjustment
[20–30 min] Month 2 plan — any changes to ICP, copy, or offer
[30–35 min] Confirm continuation / upsell conversation (if applicable)
```

**Month 1 Summary doc:**
```
MONTH 1 RESULTS

Emails sent:           [N]
Open rate:             [X]%  (target: 40%+)
Reply rate:            [X]%  (target: 3%+)
Positive replies:      [N]
Calls booked:          [N]
Clients closed:        [N]
Revenue attributed:    $[N]

WHAT WORKED:
- [Specific subject lines / audiences / angles that outperformed]

WHAT WE'RE ADJUSTING:
- [What we're changing in Month 2 and why]

MONTH 2 PLAN:
- [Volume target]
- [Copy changes]
- [New segments to test]
```

## Upsell Trigger Checklist

Look for upsell opportunities when:
- [ ] Client has booked 3+ calls from outreach
- [ ] They mention a related problem in weekly calls ("we also need content…")
- [ ] Month 1 ROI is clearly positive
- [ ] Retention is approaching 3 months

**Upsell framing:**
```
"Based on what's working, there are two ways we could accelerate this.
Option 1: [scale same service].
Option 2: [add complementary service].

Which would be more valuable for you right now?"
```

Never pitch upsells before results are proven. Upsells sell themselves when Month 1 worked.
