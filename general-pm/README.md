## How to Use This Workspace

**For thinking:** Drop your messy thoughts into Cascade. It will ask hard questions before touching any template. That's intentional.

**For execution:** Ask for a specific document. Cascade will check TEMPLATES/ and pull from your context.

**For reflection:** After shipping or after something goes wrong, update 00-META/.
