# TEMP — Staging Area

> Nothing stays here permanently. This is a dump zone.

## What Goes Here
- Meeting transcripts and notes
- Raw customer feedback
- Slack thread dumps
- Rough braindumps before structuring
- Research notes not yet synthesized

## Workflow
1. Drop file here
2. Tell Cascade what it is ("this is a transcript from a customer call")
3. Cascade extracts: decisions, action items, insights, open questions
4. Cascade identifies what to update in 01-COMPANY-CONTEXT/, 02-KNOWLEDGE/, or 03-INITIATIVES/
5. Confirm updates, then delete the TEMP file
