# 00-META — Index

> Your practice, learning, and growth system. Updated regularly — not once a year.

## What Is This Folder?
Where you track what you **do**, what you **learn**, and how your **judgment** changes over time.

## Files

| File | Purpose | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| `daily-log.md` | Quick daily capture | Daily |
| `learning-log.md` | Weekly reflections + monthly synthesis | Weekly/Monthly |
| `growth-portfolio.md` | Evidence for 1:1s, reviews, your PM narrative | Ongoing |
| `forecast-tracker.md` | Log predictions before shipping, score after | Per initiative |

## The Loop
1. Daily work → `daily-log.md`
2. Weekly step back → `learning-log.md`
3. Collect evidence → `growth-portfolio.md`
4. Log forecasts before → score outcomes after → `forecast-tracker.md`
