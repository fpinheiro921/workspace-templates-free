# Forecast Tracker

> Log your predictions BEFORE shipping. Score them AFTER outcomes resolve.
> Inspired by Brier score methodology — calibrated PMs are better PMs.

---

## How It Works
1. Before shipping/deciding: write your forecast (0–100% confidence)
2. State what you expect to happen and by when you'll know
3. After the outcome resolves: score it (Right/Wrong/Partial)
4. Look for patterns in what you over- or under-estimate
