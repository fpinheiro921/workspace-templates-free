## Status Key
- `Discovery` — problem being explored, no solution committed
- `Definition` — solution being scoped, PRD in progress
- `In Flight` — actively being built
- `Launched` — shipped, in post-launch monitoring
- `Paused` — deprioritized, not killed
- `Killed` — explicitly decided against
