# CARE Framework: DM Automation for Newsletter Growth

> Convert social media engagement into newsletter subscribers using simple DM automations. Content does 80% of the work — DMs close the sale.

**Source:** Jun — Creator College Content System Guide

---

## What This Is

A 4-step system for turning social media comments into email subscribers:

| Letter | Meaning | Purpose |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **C** — Call to Action | Tell them what to do | Drive engagement |
| **A** — Activate | Trigger the automation | Start the DM flow |
| **R** — Respond | Reply with value | Build trust |
| **E** — Enter Ecosystem | Move to your backend | Convert to subscriber |

**Core Principle:** Content does 80% of the work. DMs close the last 20%.

---

## Why DM Automations Matter

### The Problem
- You post great content
- People engage (likes, comments, saves)
- But they never leave the platform
- You don't own the relationship

### The Solution
- Add a CTA to every post
- Automate the DM response
- Deliver value immediately
- Move them to your email list

**Result:** Social followers → Email subscribers → Paid customers

## C — Call to Action

### Where to Place CTAs

**1. Feed Posts (Instagram/TikTok)**
```
Caption CTA: "Comment 'GUIDE' below and I'll DM you the
free newsletter growth checklist"
```

**2. Stories**
```
Story sticker: "Swipe up for the free guide"
Poll: "Want my newsletter growth system? Yes/Yes please"
```

**3. Reels/Shorts**
```
Verbal CTA: "Comment the word 'START' and I'll send you
my exact newsletter template"
Text overlay: "Comment 'YES' for the link"
```

**4. Lives**
```
Verbal: "Type 'GUIDE' in the comments and my bot will
DM you the link"
```

### Best-Performing CTA Types

| CTA | Best For | Conversion |
|-----|----------|------------|
| **Comment keyword** | Feed posts, Reels | Highest |
| **Link in bio** | Stories, profile | Medium |
| **DM me** | High-engagement posts | High |
| **Save this** | Evergreen content | Low (but boosts algo) |
| **Share with friend** | Relatable content | Medium (viral boost) |

### CTA Psychology

**What Works:**
- Specific keywords (not "link" — use "GUIDE")
- Low friction (one word)
- Clear outcome ("I'll DM you the checklist")
- Urgency ("First 50 people")

**What Doesn't:**
- Vague CTAs ("check out my bio")
- Multiple CTAs (confusion)
- High friction ("fill out this form")
- No incentive ("follow me")

## A — Activate

### Triggering the Automation

**How It Works:**
1. Person comments keyword (e.g., "GUIDE")
2. Automation detects the comment
3. DM sequence begins

**Tools:**
- **ManyChat** (Instagram/FB)
- **Instagram Auto-DM** (native, limited)
- **Third-party tools** (Zapier + Instagram API)

### Setting Up Keyword Triggers

**Step 1:** Choose Your Keyword
- One word, easy to spell
- Related to your offer
- Unique (not common words)

**Examples:**
- "GUIDE" for a free guide
- "START" for a starter kit
- "SYSTEM" for your method
- "LINK" for a resource

**Step 2:** Configure in ManyChat
```
Automation: "Auto-DM links from comments"
Trigger: Comment contains "GUIDE"
Action: Send DM sequence
```

**Step 3:** Test Before Going Live
- Comment the keyword from a test account
- Verify the DM sends correctly
- Check the link works
- Ensure timing feels natural

## R — Respond

### The DM Sequence

**Principles:**
- Keep it simple (2-4 messages max)
- Sound human, not robotic
- Deliver value immediately
- Clear next step in every message
- Don't overwhelm

### Basic 3-Message Flow

**Message 1 (Immediate):**
```
"Hey [name]! Here's the [resource] you asked for:
[LINK]

Let me know what you think!"
```

**Message 2 (24 hours later, if no reply):**
```
"Hey [name]! Did you get a chance to check out the
[resource]? Would love to hear your thoughts 😊"
```

**Message 3 (48 hours later, if no reply):**
```
"Quick question — what's your biggest challenge with
[topic] right now?

I'm always looking for new content ideas and would
love to help!"
```

### Advanced Nurture Flow

**For High-Value Leads:**

**Message 1:** Deliver resource
**Message 2:** Ask for feedback
**Message 3:** Share case study/testimonial
**Message 4:** Soft pitch your newsletter
**Message 5:** Final CTA with urgency

**Example:**
```
Message 1: "Here's your guide: [link]"

Message 2 (24h): "What part was most helpful?"

Message 3 (48h): "Sarah used this exact method to
get her first 1,000 subscribers in 30 days. Want to
hear how? [link to case study]"

Message 4 (72h): "I share deeper strategies like this
in my weekly newsletter. Want in? [signup link]"

Message 5 (5 days): "Last call — my newsletter goes
out tomorrow with the [specific topic]. Don't miss it:
[signup link]"
```

### DM Writing Tips

**Do:**
- Use their first name
- Keep messages under 100 words
- One CTA per message
- Match your brand voice
- Add emojis for personality

**Don't:**
- Send walls of text
- Use corporate language
- Pitch too early
- Send more than 1 message/day
- Use generic templates without customization

## E — Enter Ecosystem

### Moving From Social → Email

**The Journey:**
```
Social content → Comment CTA → DM automation → 
Landing page → Email nurture → Newsletter subscriber → Customer
```

### Landing Page Best Practices

**What to Include:**
- Headline matching the DM promise
- One clear offer (the freebie)
- Email capture form
- Social proof (testimonials, subscriber count)
- Brief explanation of what they'll get

**What to Avoid:**
- Multiple offers (confusion)
- Long forms (name + email only)
- Slow loading (mobile-first)
- Vague headlines

**Example Landing Page:**
```
Headline: "The Newsletter Growth Checklist"
Subhead: "The exact 10-step system I used to grow from
0 to 10,000 subscribers in 90 days"

Form:
[Email address] [Get the Checklist]

Social Proof: "Join 5,000+ newsletter operators"
```

### Email Nurture Sequence

**Day 0 (immediate):** Deliver the freebie
**Day 1:** Welcome + set expectations
**Day 2:** Your story/why you started
**Day 3:** Quick win (one tip they can use today)
**Day 4:** Case study/testimonial
**Day 5:** Soft pitch your newsletter
**Day 6:** FAQ or objection handling
**Day 7:** Final CTA with urgency

**Goal:** 30-50% of DM leads become newsletter subscribers.

## Platform-Specific Setup

### Instagram (ManyChat)

**Setup Steps:**
1. Connect Instagram to ManyChat
2. Create "Auto-DM links from comments" flow
3. Set keyword trigger
4. Write DM messages
5. Add link to landing page
6. Test with friend
7. Turn on and monitor

**Pro Tip:** Use Instagram Stories with "Comment" sticker to drive keyword comments.

### TikTok

**Limitation:** TikTok doesn't have native DM automation.

**Workarounds:**
- Link in bio → landing page
- "DM me on Instagram for the guide"
- Use third-party tools (Tidio, MobileMonkey)
- Build community in comments, manual DM

### YouTube

**YouTube Comments:**
- Pin a comment with CTA
- Reply to comments with link
- Use community posts for engagement
- Link in video description

## Metrics to Track

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Comment rate** | Comments / Views | 1-2% |
| **DM open rate** | Opens / DMs sent | 80%+ |
| **Landing page CTR** | Clicks / DMs | 50%+ |
| **Email capture rate** | Emails / Landing visits | 30-50% |
| **Newsletter open rate** | Opens / Sends | 40-60% |
| **Revenue per lead** | Revenue / Total leads | Track over time |

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Complex 10-Message Sequences
**✅ 2-4 messages max**

### ❌ Robotic Corporate Language
**✅ Write like you text a friend**

### ❌ Pitching in Message 1
**✅ Deliver value first, pitch later**

### ❌ Ignoring Unsubscribes
**✅ Respect opt-outs immediately**

### ❌ Set and Forget
**✅ Review metrics weekly, optimize monthly**
