# Cross-Promotion — Newsletter Swaps + Substack Recommendations

The fastest organic growth lever at 500-5k subs. Costs $0. Requires real relationships.

## The 3 formats

### 1. Substack Recommendations (Substack only)

Substack's built-in "recommend this newsletter" feature. Shows up when someone subscribes to a publication you recommend.

- Recommend 5-15 newsletters in your niche that are RELEVANT (not just big)
- Ask those publishers to recommend you back
- Check your "where are my subs coming from" dashboard — Substack Recs often becomes top channel at 500-5k subs

Rule: only recommend publications you'd actually recommend. Your reputation rides on each one.

### 2. Newsletter swap (any platform)

You mention them in your newsletter; they mention you in theirs. Same week or paired on a topic.

- Target: 1 swap per month with a newsletter at ~2-5x your subscriber count
- You provide specific ad copy (don't let them improvise)
- They provide specific ad copy for you
- Track with unique UTM links

### 3. Guest post / feature

You write a piece in their newsletter; they write one in yours. Bigger commitment, bigger payoff.

- Target: 4-6 per year
- Only with newsletters that overlap your niche but aren't direct competitors
- Link back to your newsletter in author bio

## The outreach template

```
Hey <name>,

I've been subscribed to <publication> for <N months>, and <specific thing you love about a recent issue>. The <specific angle> you took in <specific issue title> has stuck with me.

I publish <newsletter name> — <50-word description from POSITIONING/description.md Variant 3>.

I think our audiences would overlap around <specific topic>. Would you be open to a simple swap — I'd recommend you in my next issue, you'd recommend me in one of yours? Happy to go first.

Here's a recent issue if you want to sample: <link>

No pressure either way — just wanted to say thanks for the work.

— <you>
```

Rules:
- Always reference a SPECIFIC issue of theirs (proves you read them)
- Always offer to go first (lowers their effort)
- Always include a sample link (makes it easy to evaluate you)
- Never mass-send — each email personalized

## Cadence

- 1-2 outreach emails per week
- Expect 30-50% response rate (if you picked well + personalized)
- Expect 10-30% conversion to actual swap (others too busy, not the right fit, etc.)

## Ethics

- Never recommend a publication you wouldn't recommend to a friend
- Never take money for a "recommendation" and not disclose it
- If someone stops being a good fit (pivots niches, quality drops), un-recommend — your list trusts you

## Metrics

- Subs added per swap (target: 5-50)
- Subs added per Substack Recommendation (target: 10-100 over 3 months)
- Return rate on reciprocation (% of people who swap back) — should be 70%+ of those who say yes
