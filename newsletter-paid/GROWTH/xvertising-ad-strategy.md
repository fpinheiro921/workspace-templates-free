# Xvertising Ad Strategy

> Master X (Twitter) ads to create viral campaigns with ultra-low CPMs and massive earned media.

**Source:** Chris Orzechowski — Xvertising Workshop

---

## The X Advantage

- Lower competition than Facebook/Instagram
- High engagement (replies, quote tweets)
- Viral potential through earned media
- CPM as low as $0.02-$0.03

---

## The Viral Ad Formula

**1. Hook (First Line)**
- Stop the scroll
- Contrarian or emotionally charged

**2. Story/Value (Body)**
- Short, line breaks for readability
- Specific numbers and results

**3. CTA (Final Line)**
- Soft or no CTA for viral potential
- Reply in comments for link

---

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Good | Action If Bad |
|--------|------|---------------|
| CPM | <$5 | Test new creative |
| CPC | <$1 | Improve hook |
| CTR | >1% | Test angles |
| ROAS | >3x | Scale budget |

---

## 7 Ad Writing Principles

1. Lead with disruption
2. Be specific (numbers, names)
3. Write to one person
4. Use short sentences
5. Create open loops
6. Leverage social proof
7. Have a personality
