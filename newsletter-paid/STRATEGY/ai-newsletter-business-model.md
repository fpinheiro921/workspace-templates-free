# AI Newsletter Business Model

> Build a $100k/year paid newsletter using AI tools for writing, design, and subscriber acquisition.

**Source:** Ben Adkins — The $100k/Year AI-Powered Newsletter

---

## Revenue Math

| Price | Subscribers | Monthly Revenue |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| $10/mo | 833 | $8,330 |
| $20/mo | 417 | $8,340 |
| $50/mo | 167 | $8,350 |

**Formula:** Price × Subscribers × 12 = $100,000/year

## AI Writing Workflow

1. Input topic and audience to ChatGPT
2. Generate full draft
3. Human edit for voice
4. Add personal stories
5. Format for email

## Pricing Models

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | $0 | Weekly, build trust |
| Paid | $10-50/mo | Exclusive content |
| Premium | $100-500/yr | Everything + bonuses |
