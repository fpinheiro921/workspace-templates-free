## Newsletter-specific tactics

### Services as the Day-1 revenue lane
- Works from 0 subs — you don't need audience to sell services
- Fastest path to first revenue — 2 clients at $3,500/mo = $7k/mo
- Newsletter becomes the CREDIBILITY asset, not the sales channel

### Newsletter → services funnel

```
Free newsletter (builds trust, demonstrates expertise)
    ↓
Pinned newsletter features your services (see PUBLISHING/pinned-newsletter.md)
    ↓
CTA 1x/month in newsletter body — "Working with me: [link]"
    ↓
Discovery call booked via service landing page
    ↓
Client engagement
```

## Rules

- Never take a client outside your niche (see `POSITIONING/niche-framework.md`)
- Never discount for "exposure" or "testimonials"
- Case studies only from completed, successful engagements
- Contracts for every engagement

## "2 clients, not 2,000"

From `_PATTERNS/faulty-beliefs.md`:
Target: $7k/mo
Retainer: $3.5k/mo
**Clients needed: 2**

Your newsletter just needs to produce 2 people who subscribe to a service. The 1,000 other subs are the funnel.

## Newsletter content that drives services

- **Weekly value newsletter** — builds trust
- **Case study newsletter (monthly)** — demonstrates outcomes
- **Mistake + fix newsletter (bi-monthly)** — shows you understand the pain
- **Behind-the-scenes (occasional)** — humanizes + builds affinity

Avoid: overtly promotional newsletters more than 1 per month.

## CTA placement

In each newsletter, you can place a services-link in:
- Bio / header
- Footer (always)
- Body — ONLY if genuinely relevant to the issue, max 1 mention, max 1x/month

## Sales flow

For newsletter-driven services, typical sales flow:
1. Discovery call (30 min, via Calendly or similar)
2. Proposal (written, 1-page)
3. Contract + deposit (50% up front, 50% on completion for projects; monthly for retainers)
4. Kickoff call
5. Delivery

For deeper sales playbook, see `ghostwriter-practice/SALES/` when available — includes 9-step script + 7-objection bank.

## Capacity math

`Max simultaneous clients = (weekly hours available) / (hours per client per week)`

If at capacity:
1. Raise prices (not take more clients)
2. Productize (reduce hours-per-client)
3. Hire help

## Metrics

- Client LTV (lifetime value)
- Avg engagement length
- Referral rate (% of new clients from existing)
- Capacity utilization (% of available hours billed)
- Newsletter-to-discovery-call conversion (track by referrer source)
