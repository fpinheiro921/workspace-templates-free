## Newsletter-specific tactics

### Where affiliate mentions go
- **Primary sponsor slot:** top-of-newsletter, dedicated 50-100 words
- **Organic mention:** in the body when an affiliate product is relevant to the topic
- **Tools list footer:** "What I use" section, kept stable across many issues
- **Paid-tier extra:** affiliate offers exclusive to paid subs

### Disclosure language
Every affiliate placement: 
> "This is an affiliate link — I earn a commission if you sign up, at no cost to you. I only recommend products I actively use."

Once per newsletter. Consistent language.

### Frequency cap
- Primary sponsor slot: max 1 per issue
- Organic mentions: OK if genuinely relevant (never forced)
- Total affiliate links per issue: max 3 (above that, the newsletter reads like a directory)

## Metrics

- Click-through rate per placement (benchmark: 3-8%)
- Conversion rate per product (varies wildly — track baselines per partnership)
- Monthly revenue per affiliate
- Unsub delta after placements (should be 0; >0.5% = slowing down)

## Quarterly review

- Drop bottom 50% of earners (by total $)
- Pursue 1-2 new partnerships per quarter
- Kill any partnership where the product got worse / lost your trust
