## Newsletter-specific tactics

### Launch via the newsletter
- Pre-sale email to existing subs BEFORE public launch
- Founding-member pricing (first 50-100 at discount)
- Launch email sequence (5-7 emails over 7-14 days) — see `AUTOMATION/` welcome-sequence.md structure applied to launch
- Post-launch: 1 dedicated newsletter issue on the product (NOT every issue)

### Product tiers for newsletter creators

| Tier | Price | Best fit for newsletter creator |
|---|---|---|
| Entry ($10-50) | ebook / template bundle / mini-course (2-3 hours content) | Easy pre-sale to existing subs |
| Core ($100-500) | Full course / system / cohort (10-20h content or live) | Flagship product |
| Premium ($500-5k) | Coaching / intensive / done-with-you | High-ticket, 2-5 clients max |

Rule: don't build Product 2 until Product 1 is on autopilot.

## Rules

- Ship ugly, iterate. v1 doesn't need perfect branding.
- First 10 customers get white-glove support (they're your QA + testimonials)
- Newsletter stays the primary asset, NOT the product sales page. Never sacrifice newsletter quality to push a product.
- Max 1 "dedicated product" newsletter per month (otherwise the newsletter becomes a product directory)

## Launch checklist (applies to every product)

- [ ] Validated demand (5+ conversations, waitlist ≥50, or pre-sale successful)
- [ ] v1 scoped tight
- [ ] Landing page (see `saas-micro/LANDING/` or `fullstack-writer/LANDING-PAGE/` when available)
- [ ] Offer stack completed (see `creator-studio/MONETIZATION/offer-stack.md`)
- [ ] Email launch sequence drafted (5-7 emails)
- [ ] Payment processor set up (Stripe / Gumroad / Lemonsqueezy)
- [ ] Customer support flow (even if "email me")
- [ ] Refund policy (30 days, no questions)
- [ ] Delivery automated
- [ ] Post-purchase sequence tested

## Metrics

- Launch conversion (newsletter list → customer): benchmark 2-5% for a well-matched offer
- Refund rate: <5% healthy; >10% = product-market fit problem
- Repeat purchase rate across products: target 20%+ over 12 months
