## Two-Pass Review (must complete both before moving to SCHEDULED/)

### Craft Pass
- [ ] Hook lands (first 1-2 sentences are one of 6 hook types, cliffhanger-ending)
- [ ] Promise specific (stated in first 5 lines, tangible outcome)
- [ ] Structure skimmable (headers, bullets, short paragraphs)
- [ ] Takes a stance (no hedging, opinion-laden)
- [ ] CTA clear (one ask, unambiguous) OR intentionally no CTA
- [ ] Length in target range (±20%)
- [ ] 4Cs pass (clear / concise / compelling / credible)

### Voice Pass
- [ ] Banned-word audit clean (scan against `CONTEXT/voice-guide.md`)
- [ ] Rhythm alternates long + short sentences
- [ ] Punctuation style matches voice guide
- [ ] Register matches (from voice guide)
- [ ] No LLM tells ("In today's fast-paced world", "It's important to note", "Moreover", etc.)
- [ ] Contractions appropriate for voice
- [ ] Opinion-laden, not hedged
