## Quarterly review trigger

Every 90 days, revisit this file. Questions to ask:
- Is the niche still right? (If I've been on this 12+ months and not gaining traction, reconsider.)
- Is the revenue target on track? If not, which lane needs attention?
- Is my cadence sustainable? (Burnout kills more newsletters than anything else.)
- Am I still excited to write for this audience? (The single biggest predictor of success.)
