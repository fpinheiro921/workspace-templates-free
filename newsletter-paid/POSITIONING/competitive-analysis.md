# Competitive Analysis

5-10 newsletters already serving your niche (or adjacent). Know them deeply before you launch.

## Why this matters

- **To differentiate:** if 5 newsletters in your niche all take angle X, you take angle Y
- **To steal frames (ethically):** what structures, subject-line patterns, monetization models work
- **To find cross-promotion partners:** they are your future Substack Recs / swap partners, not enemies
- **To calibrate expectations:** if the biggest newsletter in your niche has 5k subs, the ceiling is probably ~50k, not 500k
