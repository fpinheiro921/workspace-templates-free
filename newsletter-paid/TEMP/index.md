# TEMP/

Raw inbox. Drop things here; Cascade extracts into the right folder later.

## What goes here

- Screenshots / photos of ideas, quotes, stats you encounter
- Transcripts from podcasts, YouTube, conversations
- Draft notes before you know what they'll become
- DMs / emails you want to save
- Research snippets
- Tweets / LinkedIn posts worth saving

## What does NOT go here

- Finished drafts (→ `DRAFTS/`)
- Ideas (→ `PUBLISHING/90-day-calendar.md` or an idea bank)
- Strategic decisions (→ `CONTEXT/decisions-log.md`)
- Subscriber notes (→ would need a dedicated subscriber-insights file)

## Cascade behavior

When user drops content into TEMP/:
1. Identify type (idea / research / swipe / DM / draft note)
2. Propose the right destination folder
3. Extract into that folder
4. Leave TEMP/ version for 24h
5. After 24h, suggest clearing

## Clearance rule

TEMP/ should be empty most of the time. If TEMP/ has >10 files, either:
- You've been dumping without processing (ritual breakdown)
- Cascade missed a processing round (flag to user)
