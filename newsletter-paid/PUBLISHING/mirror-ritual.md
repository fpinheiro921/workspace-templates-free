# Mirror Ritual — Monthly Deconstruction

First Monday of every month, pick 3 newsletters you LOVED reading that month and reverse-engineer their mechanics. Use what you learn in next month's drafts.

## Why

Viral posts aren't magic. They're structured. Reading them passively gives you vibes; deconstructing them gives you patterns you can apply.

## The method

### Step 1 — Pick 3 newsletters

Criteria:
- You opened them and read them in full (not just skimmed)
- They're from competitors or adjacent-niche publishers (see `POSITIONING/competitive-analysis.md`)
- They got visible engagement (likes, reshares, replies) OR you felt compelled to reply / forward / share

If you skipped all newsletters this month, the ritual isn't ready yet — get to 3 first.

### Step 2 — Deconstruct each

Use `creator-studio/PROMPTS/generators/mirror-prompt.md` (cross-template reference). Produces:
1. Format identification
2. Hook analysis
3. Beat-by-beat structure
4. Why it worked (3 factors)
5. Reusable template (with placeholders)
6. Adaptation warning

### Step 3 — Log

Add each deconstruction to the archive below. Tag by: angle / template / hook type.

### Step 4 — Apply

Pick ONE pattern from the 3 deconstructions to try in your next newsletter. Just one. Note which one in your next draft file.
