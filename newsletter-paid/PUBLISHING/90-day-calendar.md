## Building a quarter

### Step 1 — Pick themes

1 theme per week. Rotate pillars. For 3 pillars × 12 weeks: pillar A × 4 weeks, pillar B × 4, pillar C × 4.

### Step 2 — Pick angles

Rotate the 10 angles across the quarter. Don't publish 6 "Steps" posts in a row. Aim for each angle appearing ~once per quarter.

### Step 3 — Leave 10% slack

1 of 12 weeks = "reactive" slot. Used for: industry news, viral moment riffs, surprise guest posts.

## Weekly maintenance (Sunday, 15 min)

- Is next week's cell filled?
- Is next week's outline drafted (via `PROMPTS/generators/idea-to-outline.md`)?
- Any slots in weeks 3-6 still blank? Brainstorm + fill.

## Quarterly review (end of week 11)

- Which pillars + angles performed? (Cross-reference `PUBLISHING/analytics.md`)
- Build the NEXT quarter based on winners.
- Never copy-paste; always rebuild.

## Cascade behavior

- When user says "fill next week's slot", Cascade pulls pillar from rotation + proposes 3 angle candidates
- When user says "plan next quarter", Cascade proposes the full grid based on last quarter's winners, then asks for corrections
- Never auto-publishes; calendar entries with status "scheduled" still require Two-Pass Review before they're actually scheduled on the platform
